Frequently Asked Questions

Is TeamUp free to use?

Yes, the core features of TeamUp, like finding and joining events, are completely free. We may offer optional premium features in the future.

What sports are supported?

We initially focus on popular racquet sports like Padel and Tennis, but support various activities including Running, Soccer, Basketball, and Cycling. We plan to expand based on user demand.

How is my privacy protected?

We take privacy seriously. Your location is only used for finding nearby events if you grant permission. We don't sell your data. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I create private events?

Absolutely! You can create private, password-protected events visible only to those you invite or share the link/password with.
